Lasagna

Description:

This perfectly delicious lasagna is just waiting to be eaten.

Ingredients:

  • Cheese
  • Pasta dough
  • Veggie Beefless (c)
  • Tomato sauce

Steps:

  1. Spread the pasta dough into thin sheets
  2. Slice 4 even sheets of the pasta dough
  3. Place sheets in a stovetop boiler with boiling water
